Venator playbook

◆
Venator is a backline marksman: high burst weapon damage and lockdown utility (snap trap, gutshot) delivered from range and safety. Your win condition is to isolate and execute a target from distance without getting touched - which makes survivability and positioning, not raw damage, your real currency. This match proved it: you out-farmed the loss (1037 souls/min late) but died 9 times because a 6/1/3 split with no armor cannot survive being closed on.
Situational play
Holding a lead
Your team was +6718 at 15:00. When ahead, group and take objectives with the team instead of taking solo fights - the lead was gone by 20:00 partly because fights were being lost individually.
Against burst (Seven, Wraith)
You took 23948 from Seven and 20189 from Wraith with no armor. Slot Spirit Armor and Bullet Armor before the midgame and fight from max range so their burst windows whiff.
Late-game teamfights
You took 12533 damage and died 4 times after 28:00. Carry a survival active (Metal Skin or Cheat Death) and never be the closest target - poke from the back line and let the frontline soak.
Picking your target
You spread damage onto Seven and Yamato while Wraith (64k) ran away with the game. Always aim your output at the most-farmed enemy carry.
Macro: turning fights into objectives
  • Close out leads on objectives: your soul lead peaked at +6718 (15:00) then collapsed to -26015 by 25:00. When that far ahead, force tower and shrine pressure together rather than farming and trading lives.
  • Contest or trade the mid-boss: the enemy took mid-boss uncontested at 30:07 while you were behind. If you cannot contest it, do not be caught out of position when the enemy buff timer hits.
  • Cut your Late-phase deaths: 4 of your 9 deaths came after 28:00, when each death is longest and costs the most map control. Play the final fights from the back line and disengage the moment you are focused.
