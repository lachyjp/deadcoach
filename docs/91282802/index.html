Warden playbook

◆
Warden is the durable frontline initiator: his value is surviving on the front, locking a squishy with Flask + Binding Word, and grinding them down while Last Stand keeps him alive. This game you built him as a pure weapon carry (7 weapon items, 0 armour) - that throws away his entire identity. The numbers prove it: you out-farmed your rank but took 35,374 damage and died 9 times because you were as fragile as a true carry but without their range. Build him tanky and he becomes the problem instead of the victim.
Situational play
Losing the lane
You were -3,580 by 9:00 and took 5,318 damage in lane; when trades go 2:1 against you, buy Healing Rite/sustain and an armour component and farm safely rather than forcing fights like the 35.2s death at 8:49.
The 12-18 min armour window
By 18:00 you had zero armour and had died 4 times. This is the make-or-break window - Bullet Armor and Spirit Armor here would have flipped the 3,834-taken-to-1,197-dealt early-mid trades.
Picking a target in a fight
You poured 18,016 into Abrams (a bruiser you killed twice) while Lady Geist (25,605 to you) ran free. Flask + Binding Word the highest-net-worth squishy instead.
When behind in the late game
Four late deaths deep on the enemy side (e.g. 36:35, 37:49) at 56-82s downtime each let your base fall. When 30k+ down, group and defend off your own structures with Last Stand from cover - don't chase souls into enemy territory.
Macro: turning fights into objectives
  • Contest or at least deny the mid-boss: the enemy took it uncontested twice (24:02 and 34:18) while you farmed, and both fed directly into their push. When you hear the mid-boss being taken, rotate or trade an objective elsewhere.
  • Your late deaths map to lost structures: dying at 36:35 (-1439,-4168) and 37:49 (-280,-7846) deep on the enemy side directly preceded objectives 9-15 falling between 36:16 and 37:25. Stop extending when behind and defend off your own guardians.
  • Respect the soul-lead chart: you were behind from 6:00 and never level. Once you're 8k+ down (20:00), stop taking the greedy farm/fights deep in enemy jungle (deaths at 17:19 and 26:10) and play for picks and defensive objectives instead.
  • You secured one objective bounty (996 souls mid, a 308 boss bounty) - good, but those were solo farm, not team objectives. Convert your strong farm into grouped pushes with the team rather than split-farming a losing map.
